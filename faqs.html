Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know about our precision detailing services and obsidian-grade protection.

How long does a full detail take? add

Service times vary by package. An Interior Detail typically takes 2–3 hours, a Full Detail 3–5 hours, and our Diamond package (which includes paint correction) can take 6–8+ hours. Ceramic coating applications require an additional full day. We'll always give you a precise time estimate when you book.

What is ceramic coating and how long does it last? add

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that chemically bonds to your vehicle's paint, creating a permanent protective layer with a 9H hardness rating. Unlike wax, it doesn't wash away. Depending on the product tier, our coatings last 2–3 years (Crystal+), 6 years (Pro+), or a lifetime (Max G+). All our coatings are SystemX certified.

Do you come to my location? add

Yes — we are 100% mobile. We bring all equipment, water, and power to your driveway, garage, or workplace. We service the entire South Shore including Scituate, Marshfield, Duxbury, Plymouth, Hingham, and surrounding towns. No need to drop your car off anywhere.

What products and chemicals do you use? add

We use only professional-grade, pH-balanced products from industry-leading brands. For ceramic coatings we are authorized SystemX applicators. All products are safe for your vehicle's surfaces, paint, leather, and trim. We never use harsh detergents that could strip sealants or damage clear coat.

Is paint correction necessary before ceramic coating? add

Paint correction is strongly recommended before ceramic coating. Ceramic locks in the current condition of your paint — if there are swirl marks, scratches, or oxidation, the coating will preserve those defects permanently. Our Diamond package includes paint correction specifically for this reason. We'll assess your paint during the consultation and advise accordingly.

What areas do you service? add

We primarily serve the South Shore of Massachusetts, including Scituate, Marshfield, Duxbury, Plymouth, Hingham, Cohasset, Norwell, and Pembroke. For commercial fleet work or larger ceramic coating projects we can travel further. Contact us to confirm coverage for your specific location.

Do you detail boats and marine vessels? add

Absolutely — marine detailing is one of our specialties. We offer dock-side service for everything from powerboats to sailboats. Packages include the Mariner's Detail, Captain's Detail (most popular), and full Ceramic Coating. We also offer on-dock maintenance programs billed per linear foot for seasonal care.

What is your warranty and aftercare policy? add

All ceramic coating applications come with a manufacturer-backed warranty through SystemX. Our Pro+ coating carries a 6-year warranty and Max G+ is lifetime-warranted. We provide detailed aftercare instructions and recommend maintenance washes every 4–6 weeks to preserve the coating. Clean Car Club members receive quarterly maintenance top-ups to keep the coating performing at its peak.
